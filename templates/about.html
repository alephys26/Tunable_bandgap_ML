Welcome to MaxMin Frequency Predictor - Your Pinonic Crystal Band Gap Assistant!

About Us:

Welcome to MaxMin Frequency Predictor, your trusted destination for predicting the band gap frequencies of pinonic crystals. Our cutting-edge regression model accurately forecasts the maximum and minimum frequencies (max1, min1, max2, min2) of these crystals, providing valuable insights into their unique properties.

Meet the Team:

We are a team of dedicated researchers and data scientists passionate about unraveling the mysteries of pinonic crystals. The core foundation of our platform lies in the exceptional research conducted by Dr. Atul Kumar Sharma,(https://sites.google.com/iitj.ac.in/atul-kumar-sharma) whose exclusive dataset shared with us has been instrumental in training our ML model. Dr. Sharma's work on "Topology optimization of soft compressible phononic laminates for widening the mechanically tunable band gaps" (ISSN 0263-8223, https://doi.org/10.1016/j.compstruct.2022.115389) has been a guiding force in our approach. Additionally, we have received valuable guidance from Dr. Harshal Akholekar for ML-related issues (https://www.harshalakolekar.com/).

Our Mission:
